Elon Musk

Elon Reeve Musk ( born June 28, 1971 in Pretoria , South Africa ) is a South African native and primarily based in the United States but also global entrepreneur . In the context of his diverse ventures, he is best known as a co-owner, technical director and partly also co-founder of the payment service PayPal as well as head of the aerospace company SpaceX and the electric car manufacturer Tesla . He is one of the richest people in the world with a total fortune of approximately 200 billion US dollars .

Musk has both South African and Canadian citizenship by birth , and in 2002 he also received US citizenship.

Entrepreneurial Activities

Zip2 (1995

--> Main article : Zip2

In 1995, Musk co-founded his first company, Zip2 , with his brother Kimbal, providing content for media companies. According to Musk, at the time he had "a vague idea of ​​the opportunity that a dot -com company, $2,000 in capital, a car and a computer could offer." When computer maker Compaq bought the company for $307 million in 1999, was this the highest price paid for an Internet company up to that point; Musk himself was then able to access $22 million in capital through his stake.


X.com and PayPal (1999/2000)

--> Main articles : X.com and PayPal

Immediately afterwards, in 1999, Musk founded the company X.com , which developed an online payment system via e-mail . In 2000, X.com merged with competitor Confinity , which specialized in a similar product called PayPal . [33] In the years that followed, PayPal became the most important online payment system in the world - and achieved sales proceeds of 1.5 billion US dollars when it was sold to eBay in 2002 . Musk held 11.7% of the company's shares at the time, making it the largest shareholder. [34] In July 2017, Musk reacquired the x.com domain from PayPal.

While Musk's Internet company opened up new markets with products that did not previously exist in this form, the business idea that was to underlie the entrepreneurial activities of the following years was different: Musk wanted expensive and technically complex products that were cheaper and suitable for mass production offer.


SpaceX (2002)

--> Main article : SpaceX

Musk's third founding was the space company SpaceX in 2002 . He is CEO and chief rocket designer there. Within 15 years, SpaceX has become the world's leading commercial provider of orbital rocket launches, especially for the transport of satellites into orbit, thanks to high cost-efficiency. In addition, SpaceX has been supplying the ISS space station with the Dragon spaceship since 2012 . With the successor Crew Dragon , the first manned flight to the ISS took place in May 2020. [37] Russia accused Musk of deliberately trying to force Russian space transporters out of the market with discount prices.

SpaceX's goal is to reduce the cost of space transportation to the point where it will be possible to settle people on other celestial bodies - especially Mars. [40] A first groundbreaking step on this path was the establishment of a landable and reusable rocket stage in 2017. The second major step towards affordable space travel is to take place with the fully reusable Starship rocket , which is under development . Long-term, flights for Martian settlers are targeted for under $500,000 per person.
